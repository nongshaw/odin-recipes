Lasagna


Description

Lasagna is delicious. My mum makes a neat lasagna. I've never asked for her recipe, but I have a feeling she just wings it and nails it every time.

So yeah, I don't actually know how to make it. But I just need content here, so we'll be alright I reckon.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Beef mince
  • Onion
  • Lasagna sauce (white)

Method

  1. Pre-heat the oven to... oh, I don't know. 180 degrees celsius.
  2. Cook the onions.
  3. Cook the beef.
  4. Lay down the first pasta sheet.
  5. Pour a thin layer of white sauce.
  6. Pour a thin layer of cooked beef and onions.
  7. Repeat steps 3-5 at least three times, until you run out of contents.
  8. Chuck it in the over. Who knows how long. 20 minutes?
