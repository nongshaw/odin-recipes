Chicken Curry


Description

You might be confused by the name of this recipe. Chicken curry? But what type of curry? Green, red? Is it Indian? Is it Malaysian? To which I say, please do not follow this recipe. I'm just in the mood for some chicken, and for a curry. So here we are, and here we go.

I did cook something like this a week or two ago and it didn't quite turn out the way I wanted, but it was still delicious as.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast
  • 1 Brown onion
  • Rice - I choose Jasmine Rice, but it's up to taste
  • Curry sauce

Method

  1. Cook the onions in a frying pan with olive oil.
  2. Once the onions are browned, add the chicken.
  3. Cook until browned and cooked through. Add the curry sauce. Yes, from a tin, or a glass bottle. It's legit. Though not cost effective. And hardly requires a recipe.
